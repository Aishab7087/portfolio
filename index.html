Bridging Data Science & Business Intelligence

With a foundation in Chemical Engineering (BTech, MIT World Peace University, Pune), I developed strong analytical, problem-solving, and process-thinking skills that translate naturally into data science. The rigour of engineering — modelling complex systems, validating results, and optimising processes — gave me a methodical approach to working with data before I formally transitioned into the field.

I am completing an MSc in Data Science at Nottingham Trent University, where I applied my learning directly in industry. At Essilor Luxottica, I used Databricks, PySpark, and Power BI to build operational dashboards and transform large-scale warehouse datasets — turning raw data into actionable business insight for senior stakeholders. Prior to this, I spent two years as a Technical Analyst in IAM and SaaS environments, gaining cross-functional experience across global enterprise teams.

My goal is to join a data-driven team as a Data or BI Analyst, where I can apply my technical toolkit and cross-functional communication skills to help organisations make smarter decisions. I am passionate about the space where clean data, meaningful visualisation, and real business impact meet.
